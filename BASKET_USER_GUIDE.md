# 🛍️ How to Use Your Beige.AI Shopping Basket

## Quick Start (60 seconds)

### Step 1: Get a Recommendation
1. Fill in your mood, weather, and preferences
2. Click **"Generate Cake Recommendation"**
3. View your personalized recommendations

### Step 2: Add Items to Basket
1. Click **"✓ Add to Basket"** under any recommendation
2. See the item appear in the sidebar basket (left side)
3. Notice the toast notification `✅ Added!`

### Step 3: Checkout
1. Click **"💳 Complete Purchase"** in the sidebar basket
2. Review your order in the summary
3. Click **"✓ Confirm & Complete"**
4. See your confirmation message
5. Done! Your basket automatically clears

---

## Where Is My Basket?

**Your basket is always in the left sidebar** under "🛍️ Your Selection"

It shows:
- ✓ Each cake you added
- ✓ Individual price
- ✓ Item count
- ✓ Running total
- ✓ Remove (✕) buttons
- ✓ Checkout button

## What If...?

### Basket looks empty?
**Your basket IS empty!** Add an item by clicking any "Add to Basket" button.

### Can't see the sidebar?
**Refresh the page** or maximize your browser window. The sidebar should be on the left.

### Total doesn't match my items?
**Refresh the page.** Prices come from our database. Sometimes there's a sync delay.

### Want to start over?
**Click ✕ next to each item** to remove them, or **complete the purchase** to auto-clear.

### How many items can I add?
**As many as you want!** Add unlimited items to your basket.

## FAQ

**Q: Is my basket saved if I close the browser?**  
A: No. Your basket is only saved during your current browsing session.

**Q: Can I change quantities?**  
A: Not yet. Add the same cake multiple times if you want more.

**Q: Why did the total change?**  
A: Prices are pulled from our database. Refresh to sync.

**Q: Can I see my past orders?**  
A: View the analytics dashboard to see all historical sales.

**Q: What if an item is out of stock?**  
A: You'll see a warning at checkout. Choose a different item.
